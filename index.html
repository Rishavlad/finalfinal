About Veterans Cricket Nepal

Our Mission

Veterans Cricket Nepal (VCAN) is a dedicated organization committed to promoting cricket among veteran players in Nepal. We believe that age is just a number when it comes to the passion for cricket, and we provide a platform for experienced players to continue their love for the game.

Our Vision

To create a thriving community of veteran cricketers who can share their experience, mentor young players, and continue to contribute to the development of cricket in Nepal. We aim to bridge the gap between generations of cricketers and preserve the rich tradition of the sport.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in everything we do
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty and fairness in all our activities
  • Community: Building strong relationships among veteran cricketers
  • Mentorship: Sharing knowledge and experience with the next generation
